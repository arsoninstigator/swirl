Resources

If you're a parent and have any questions regarding the program and any events we have planned, feel free to email help@codeandcipher.xyz with your concerns.

If you feel like you didn't understand something that was done during the meeting, send a message on the WhatsApp group and let your peers help you out! If you feel like you're still stuck, feel free to email me at anne@codeandcipher.xyz so I can take a look at the content and revise it again next week (either in class itself or when you hang back afterwards).

If you're an industry professional or a college student and want to host an event, feel free to drop an email and we'll figure something out. If you're willing to fund any activities we have planned, please visit our donations page.
