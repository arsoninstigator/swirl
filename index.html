Welcome to Code & Cipher

A community-driven organization dedicated to tech education, innovation, and empowerment.

About Us

Code & Cipher is an after-school program based in New Delhi, India (but also operating virtually) dedicated to introducing young women (middle school aged girls in particular) to the exciting world of technology. We help financially underprivileged girls from government schools write their first line of code and nudge them in the direction of STEM. Code & Cipher is committed to fostering the next generation of tech leaders through workshops, events, and collaborative learning. We provide a platform for young girls of all backgrounds to engage with technology in a supportive and innovative environment.

Eligibility

If you're a female under the age of 19, club registration for you is open all year round. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis and becoming a member is completely free! Summer general interest meeting timings will be announced soon on the website.
